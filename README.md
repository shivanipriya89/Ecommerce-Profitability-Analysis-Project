This is a Data Analytics Project on Ecommerce Profitability 
Analysis of an Ecommerce Company 
E-Commerce Profitability Analysis Case Study where South Region contributes to 
25.14% of Sales,electronics category has 31.4% of Profit with negative trendline of Sales

Business Problem:
An e-commerce company has high sales but inconsistent profits. Management wants to understand:
- What drives revenue
- Why profitability is low
- Which customers/products contribute most
- How to improve business performance

Dataset:
Table: orders
Columns:
order_id, customer_id, order_date, region, category, product, sales, quantity, discount, profit

Project Sections & Questions:

1. Data Cleaning (Python)
- Convert order_date to datetime
- Check missing values
- Remove duplicates
- Validate data types

2. Exploratory Data Analysis
- Total sales and profit
- Sales by region
- Sales by category
- Monthly sales trend

3. Profitability Analysis
- Region with highest profit margin
- Most profitable category
- Loss-making products
- Profit margin calculation (profit/sales)

4. Discount Impact Analysis
- Does higher discount reduce profit?
- Compare profit across discount ranges
- Optimal discount range

5. Customer Analysis
- Top 10 customers by sales
- Contribution % of top customers
- Repeat customers

6. SQL Analysis
- Total sales by region
- Total sales by category
- Top customers
- Monthly trend
- Rank customers (RANK)
- Row number per customer

7. Statistical Analysis
- Mean vs Median
- Outliers using IQR
- Correlation (discount vs profit, quantity vs sales)

8. Visualization
- Bar chart (region sales)
- Line chart (monthly trend)
- Heatmap (correlation)
- Boxplot (outliers)

9. Insights
- Write 5 key business insights

10. Recommendations
- Improve profitability
- Optimize discounting
- Focus categories/customers

Deliverables:
- Python notebook
- SQL queries
- Dashboard
- Insights & recommendations
